Auction Rules

Please read the following rules carefully before participating in any auctions:

  • Participants must register before placing bids.
  • Bids must be placed in whole amounts and must be higher than the previous bid.
  • Each auction has a set end time; bids placed after the end time are not considered.
  • Winning bids are subject to payment within 24 hours of the auction closing.
  • All items are sold as-is. No returns or exchanges are accepted.
  • Disputes will be resolved based on the auction platform's terms and conditions.
